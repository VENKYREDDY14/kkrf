EV Charging Station Management Systems

Reduce electricity costs and get advanced diagnostics on station health to keep it up and running. Grow your charging infrastructure and deliver exceptional experiences to customers with effortless tracking, management, and optimization of EV charging.

Parking Detection and Management Systems

Our parking management solutions use advanced sensors and machine learning algorithms to detect vehicle presence, optimize parking space utilization, and provide real-time availability data. These systems integrate seamlessly with EV charging infrastructure to create a comprehensive mobility solution that improves user experience and maximizes resource efficiency.

V2G Systems

Our Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) systems enable bidirectional energy flow between electric vehicles and the power grid. This technology allows EV owners to sell excess energy back to the grid during peak demand periods while optimizing charging during low-demand times. Our V2G solutions include sophisticated control algorithms, grid integration interfaces, and user-friendly management tools to maximize financial and environmental benefits.

Demand Response Systems

Our demand response systems for EV charging networks intelligently adjust charging patterns in response to grid conditions, energy prices, and user preferences. These systems help prevent grid overload during peak demand periods while ensuring vehicles are charged when needed. By participating in utility demand response programs, charging network operators can generate additional revenue while contributing to grid stability.

Energy Management Systems (EMS)

Our Energy Management Systems (EMS) optimize power distribution across charging stations, building systems, and renewable energy sources. These comprehensive solutions monitor real-time energy usage, predict demand patterns, and implement dynamic load balancing to prevent circuit overloads and minimize demand charges. Our EMS integrates with solar arrays, battery storage, and building management systems to create holistic energy ecosystems.

Connectivity (OCPP, OCPI, OICP & more)

We provide comprehensive connectivity solutions that ensure your charging stations can communicate using industry-standard protocols including Open Charge Point Protocol (OCPP), Open Charge Point Interface (OCPI), Open InterCharge Protocol (OICP), and more. Our expertise enables seamless integration with multiple back-end systems, roaming platforms, and payment providers, giving your customers access to a wider charging network while simplifying management and billing processes.

Smart Charging

Our smart charging solutions use advanced algorithms to optimize charging schedules based on grid capacity, energy prices, renewable energy availability, and user preferences. These systems can automatically adjust charging power and timing to minimize costs while ensuring vehicles are ready when needed. Smart charging capabilities include peak shaving, load balancing across multiple stations, and integration with time-of-use electricity rates.

EV Telematics Integration

Our EV telematics integration solutions enable seamless communication between vehicles, charging stations, and management systems. By collecting and analyzing data from vehicle onboard systems, we provide insights into battery health, charging patterns, and energy consumption. This integration enables features such as automatic charging station authentication, pre-conditioning during charging, and smart route planning based on charging infrastructure availability.

eScooter App Development

We design and develop comprehensive mobile applications for electric scooter sharing services that provide seamless user experiences from registration to ride completion. Our eScooter apps include features such as real-time scooter location tracking, unlocking mechanisms, route mapping, payment processing, and ride history. The platforms integrate with backend management systems for fleet monitoring, maintenance scheduling, and operational analytics.

EV Battery Management System

Our EV Battery Management Systems (BMS) maximize battery performance, lifespan, and safety through advanced monitoring and control technologies. These systems continuously track battery cell parameters, optimize charging and discharging processes, and implement protective measures against harmful conditions. Our BMS solutions include software interfaces for remote monitoring, diagnostic tools for predictive maintenance, and data analytics for performance optimization and longevity assessment.
