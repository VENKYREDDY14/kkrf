Our Journey

What started as a small team of passionate developers has evolved into a global technology powerhouse. Our journey has been defined by innovation, resilience, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

2013

Foundation

KKRF was founded with a vision to provide innovative technological solutions to businesses of all sizes. We started with a small team of 5 developers working from a tiny office space.

2016

Expansion

With growing demand for our services, we expanded our operations to 3 major cities and reached a milestone of 50 team members. Our client portfolio grew to include several Fortune 500 companies.

2019

Global Presence

We established our international headquarters and expanded to 5 countries. Our team grew to over 100 members with expertise spanning across various technology domains.

2022

Innovation Hub

We launched our Innovation Hub, a dedicated research center focused on emerging technologies like AI, blockchain, and IoT. This initiative positioned us at the forefront of technological advancement.

2024

Today

Today, KKRF stands as a leader in the technology industry with over 200 professionals worldwide, serving clients across diverse industries and continents. Our commitment to excellence remains unchanged.
